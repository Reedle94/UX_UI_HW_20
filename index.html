Fish Flavor Profiles

Yummo!

Fish are great. Let's learn about them!

King Salmon
  • The largest species of salmon, growing up to 58 inches long.
  • Has a dense meaty texture and a fatty consistency. Often, the fish is used for sushi dishes.
  • Can be paired with many ingredients but is often paired with soy sauce, capers and miso.
Halibut
  • The largest flatfish.
  • A lean white fish with a mild, sweet taste.
  • Pairs well with basil, lemon and pesto.
Trout
  • A freshwater fish that always returns to the same part of a river where they were hatched to spawn.
  • A fish with a nut-like flavor that is tender and soft.
  • Goes well with thyme, paprika, rosemary, butter and lemon.
Rockfish
  • More than 100 different rockfish species exist, varying in color, size and shape.
  • This delicious white fish has a flaky texture and a subtle nutty flavor.
  • Rockfish is commonly prepared with a handful of ingredients, including lemon, butter and capers.
